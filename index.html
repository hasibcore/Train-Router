System Vision
What is TrainRouter BD?
TrainRouter BD

Smart Bangladesh Train Route & Fare Enquiry System

TrainRouter BD is a specialized web application for Bangladesh Railway passengers. Users can enter their origin and destination ("From-To") to instantly check active trains, available seat counts across seat classes, fare rates in BDT (৳), and calculate total journey costs.

  • From-To Search: Discover all available intercity express trains on selected routes
  • Live Seat Inventory: Track available seats for Shovon, Snigdha, AC Berth & AC Cabin
  • Seat-wise Fare Calculator: Dynamic BDT (৳) fare computation according to seat class & passengers

Background Context
Challenges in Railway Commute Planning
TrainRouter BD

Uncertain Train Availability

Passengers often don't know how many trains are running between specific stations (e.g. Dhaka to Cox's Bazar or Sylhet) or their departure schedules.

Lack of Seat Class Details

Commuters struggle to check real-time seat availability for specific classes like Shovon Chair, Snigdha, AC Berth, or AC Cabin before visiting stations.

Complex Fare Calculation

Calculating total journey fare for multiple passengers based on seat category and distance requires manual formulas, causing budget confusion.

BD Calculation Rules
Bangladesh Railway Fare & Duration Engine
TrainRouter BD

BD Dynamic Fare Formula (BDT ৳)

Seat Fare = (Base Rate × Route KM) × Seat Class Multiplier + 15% VAT

Estimated Journey Duration

Duration = (Distance / Train Speed) + Junction Delay Buffer (Tongi, Joydebpur, Akhaura)

Seat Multipliers (BR Rate Matrix)

  • Shovon Chair (S_CHAIR): 1.0x (Standard Base Tariff)
  • Snigdha (AC Chair): 1.8x (Air-Conditioned Premium)
  • AC Berth (AC_BATH): 2.5x (Night Sleeper Berth)
  • AC Cabin (AC_CABIN): 2.8x (Private Compartment)

Feature 01
From-To & Date-Based Train Availability Search
TrainRouter BD

From-To Station & Date Selection

Passengers select origin (From), destination (To), and journey date to discover all available intercity express trains running on that exact date.

  • Station Dropdown: Select From (e.g., Dhaka Kamalapur) and To (e.g., Chattogram) with autocomplete
  • Date Picker & Off-Day Filter: Filters out weekly off-day trains (e.g., Suborno OFF on Wed) automatically
  • Available Train Counter: Instantly displays total running intercity trains (e.g., "4 Trains Available on 24 July")

TrainRouter BD

Date-Wise Available Train List

Feature 06
Ticket Store & Departure Alarming Pin
TrainRouter BD

Ticket Pinned Storage & Journey Alarm Pin

Commuters can pin and store their paper or online train ticket details. On the journey day, the app acts as an active alarming pin and departure notification system.

  • Ticket Details Storage: Store ticket photo, reference pin, train name, coach/seat info, and departure schedule locally.
  • Journey Day Alarming Pin: Triggers sound alarms & notifications (e.g. 2 hours before departure, station boarding alert).
  • Offline Quick Pass: Access pinned ticket information at platform gates without requiring an active internet connection.
